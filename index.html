We believe that education is not just all about having a great classroom with amazing tutors and brilliant text books that can aide a child in scoring great results. A well-equipped computer lab with trained faculties from NIIT is present to train and clear any doubts patiently. The high-tech auditorium here at RNS can house a capacity of 1000 and includes a world class projector, sound system and extensive lighting. There is also a fully fledged transport system with 6 buses that comes with skilled and experienced drivers and tolerant helpers to help toddlers get in and get out the bus as well as help them get across the street safely. With the state of the art classrooms, well edited text books from highly versed reknown authors and play grounds and libraries that is bound to help in the growth of a child, both in personality and in physicality, we make sure that each student enrolled here with us gets the equal exposure to cope up with any hardships they might need to face with the outside world.   • LNational High Jump record

    Pavana Nagaraj, Class X student of Bunts' Sangha RNS Vidyaniketan, Bengaluru set a remarkable record on the opening day of the 36th National Junior Athletics Championship held at Guwahati, India on 1st week of February 2021 by claiming the Gold Medal and by rewriting the Under 16 National High Jump record of 1.73 Metres. Pavana, daughter of Senior National record holder and a former Olympic participant, Smt.Sahana Kumari and B G Nagaraj, an acclaimed sprinter has continued her family traditions of setting up new records each time by following the illustrious footsteps of her mother with complete determination and dedication.
